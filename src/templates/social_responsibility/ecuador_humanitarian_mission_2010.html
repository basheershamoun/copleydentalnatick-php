Medical Missions for Children Quito 2010

Our amazing team of volunteer surgeons, nurses, anesthesiologists and dentists screened 150 patients. Team members performed surgical procedures to children.

Our dental team headed by Dr. Charles Badaoui, (pictured with patients) also provided free dental care and other procedures to children in the clinic at Padre Pedro Carollo Hospital and donated much needed equipment.

To learn more, please visit the Medical Missions for Children website.

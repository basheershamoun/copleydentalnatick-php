Antigua Guatemala Humanitarian Mission 2011

During our one week humanitarian mission to Guatemala, Medical Missions For Children( MMFC) team members performed 75 surgical procedures to children, 50 of which were cleft lip and palate procedures and 25 of which were microtia (re-construction of the outer ear). Our dental team headed by Dr. Charles Badaoui, (pictured below with a patient) also provided free dental care and other procedures to 63 children in the clinic at Hermano Pedro Hospital and donated much needed equipment.

To learn more, please visit the Medical Missions for Children website.

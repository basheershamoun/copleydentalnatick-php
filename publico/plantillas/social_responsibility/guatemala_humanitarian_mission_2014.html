Medical Missions for Children Guatemala 2014

During our one week humanitarian mission to Guatemala, Medical Missions For Children( MMFC) team members performed several surgical procedures to children, including cleft lip and palate procedures and microtia (re-construction of the outer ear). Our dental team headed by Dr. Charles Badaoui, (pictured below with a patient) also provided free dental care and other procedures to several children in the clinic and donated much needed equipment.

To learn more, please visit the Medical Missions for Children website.

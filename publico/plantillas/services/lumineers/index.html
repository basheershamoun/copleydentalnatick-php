Lumineers

Lumineers by Cerinate are porcelain veneers that offer a painless way to a whiter and perfectly aligned smile. At Copley Dental, we can apply these contact-lens-thin "smile shapers" to teeth — in many cases, without any grinding or shaving — whitening and transforming teeth into a naturally beautiful smile.
Unlike traditional veneers, Lumineers are contact-lens-thin and are placed over existing teeth without having to remove painful tooth structures, achieving a beautiful, whiter smile.
Lumineers are a painless, cosmetic solution for stained, chipped, discolored or misaligned teeth.

Viviera Retainers

Maintain your new smile with Vivera Retainers

It took time, skill and investment to create your confident new smile. To make sure it lasts for years to come, consider using Vivera retainers.

Importance of retention

Studies show that without retention even aligned teeth can gradually revert back towards their initial position. This is a very common problem with all orthodontic treatments, but it is one that can easily be overcome. Wearing Vivera retainers means you can protect your smile – now and in the future.

Why choose Vivera retainers?

Vivera retainers are made with the same state-of-the-art technology as Invisalign aligners. This means that by using Vivera retainers to maintain your new smile, you benefit from some of the same qualities found in our revolutionary aligners.

Comfort through technologyVivera retainers are made with the same state-of-the-art technology as Invisalign.
RemovableVivera retainers are removable, helping you to maintain your overall oral hygiene and function.
Superior StrengthLab tests show that Vivera retainers are up to 30% stronger* when compared to other leading clear retainer Materials. This is a critical quality for keeping your smile in its final position.
Clean & ClearClear retainers can get dull and dirty over time. With Vivera retainers you receive three sets of retainers so you can switch to a clean and fresh one every few months.

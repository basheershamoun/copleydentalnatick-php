In the past I’d considered having some treatment, but with my public facing job as a police officer, there was no way I was going to feel okay about having bulky, fixed metal braces in my mouth. I decided to look into the different treatment options available.

Now I smile for photos again

What impressed me most about the Invisalign treatment was how it easily fitted into my lifestyle. I could wear them at work and you could barely see them. This meant I could be confident in any given situation. And because the aligners were removable, I was able to take them out for my sister’s wedding day – which I never would have been able to do with other braces.

Now the treatment is over I’m overjoyed with the results. I now smile for any photo that I’m in – birthdays, holidays and, yes, weddings.
